A Wine Experience Out Of The Ordinary

Wine is at the heart of Moldovan life - even the shape of the country resembles a bunch of grapes dropped onto the map. Moldova has a little over 100,000 hectares of vines which actually means that the country has a bigger proportion of its land covered with vineyards than any other country.
